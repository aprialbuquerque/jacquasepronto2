  • Fun things to do in Rio de Janeiro:

Rio de Janeiro is home to some of the best forró and samba de gafieira venues in Brazil.



Tips for a Memorable Experience


1. Beach Day at Copacabana: Spend a day soaking up the sun and enjoying the vibrant atmosphere at Copacabana Beach. Whether you're lounging on the sand or trying your hand at beach volleyball, Copacabana offers endless opportunities for fun and relaxation.


2. Hike to the Top of Sugarloaf Mountain: Take in breathtaking panoramic views of Rio de Janeiro from the top of Sugarloaf Mountain. Hiking to the summit is a thrilling adventure, and the stunning vistas of the city, ocean, and surrounding mountains make it well worth the effort.


3. Take Samba and Forró Classes at Jaime Arôxa Copacabana Dance School: Immerse yourself in the vibrant rhythms of Brazilian dance by taking Samba and Forró classes at Jaime Arôxa Copacabana Dance School. Located in the heart of Copacabana, this renowned dance school offers expert instruction in two of Brazil's most iconic dance styles. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, these classes are sure to add a new level of excitement to your Rio experience.


4. Explore the Historic Streets of Santa Teresa: Wander through the charming cobblestone streets of Santa Teresa and discover its eclectic mix of art galleries, restaurants, and colorful murals. Be sure to ride the iconic yellow tram, known as the "bonde," for a nostalgic journey through this bohemian neighborhood.


5. Sunset Cruise on Guanabara Bay: Experience the beauty of Rio's coastline from the water with a sunset cruise on Guanabara Bay. Sip on caipirinhas as you sail past iconic landmarks like Christ the Redeemer and Sugarloaf Mountain, and watch as the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky in stunning hues of orange and pink.


With so many exciting activities to choose from, your time in Rio de Janeiro is sure to be unforgettable. From dancing the night away to exploring the city's natural beauty, there's something for everyone to enjoy in this vibrant and lively destination.

Luiz Bragança

Luiz Bragança: Forró teacher, avid anime and cinema enthusiast.

Luiz Bragança has had a solid and long career in dance, which began when he was a teenager. Over the years, he has traveled and lived in various parts of the world, teaching Forró, samba de Gafieira, and other rhythms. His seasons included stints in Egypt, Switzerland, Italy, and various regions of Europe.

Additionally, Luiz Bragança speaks English and French fluently, and he loves teaching tourists due to the cultural and linguistic diversity.

Currently, Luiz teaches group and private classes at the Jaime Arôxa Copacabana Dance School.
